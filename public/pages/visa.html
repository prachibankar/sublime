About Visa

Visa is required of any traveller who intends to enter a foreign country. Visa is a kind of approval given to a person from the immigration body of a country before entering its boundaries. The immigration authority of a country goes through all the previous background , future plans and objectives of visiting the country of traveller and permits him to enter in its area. Visa procedure and mode of stamping varies nation to nation. Some countries like the USA, Canada, Germany have visa stamping procedures while some like thailand, Malaysia have e-visa procedures too.

Types of Visa

Visas are categorized on the basis of purpose of travel. The most popular category of visas are:

Business Visa

A business visa is meant for getting involved in business relations and meetings overseas which does not gain any kind of employment. It can be a business summit.

Student Visa

If a student gets admitted to an abroad institution, endorsement student visa allows the students to get enrolled at foreign qualified institutions.

Tourist Visa

If one is intending to visit a country for self amusement visa category will be considered as a Tourist visa . It's a permit that allows a person to enter a particular country for leisure purposes.
